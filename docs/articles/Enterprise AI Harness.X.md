Self-Hosted Enterprise AI Harnesses

The problem is not the components. It is the boundaries.

Identity, runtimes, routing, orchestration, execution, and secret management. The ecosystem already has the building blocks. The hard part is at the seams.

How does identity survive all the way to an MCP call? Where does runtime stop and execution begin?

I’d love to open a discussion about what Enterprise AI Harnesses should look like.
